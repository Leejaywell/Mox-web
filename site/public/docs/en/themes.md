# Themes & Effects

Mox ships **20 themes**, each with its own ambient background effect (rendered live by the Metal GPU), plus a breathing "Animal Island" living scene.

## Switching themes

- "Themes" (top-right) opens the appearance panel (Light / Dark groups) — click any theme to re-skin the whole window live.
- Your choice is remembered across launches.

## Light themes (11)

Classic, Red Maple, Lilac Haze, Liquid Glass, Ink, Celadon, Japandi, Bamboo, and three islands: Animal Island, Sakura Island, Snow Island.

## Dark themes (9)

Graphite, Midnight, Dracula, Ember, Frost Night, Firefly Night, Deep Sea, Listening Rain, Crimson Night.

## Animal Island living scene

Island themes skip particles for a full 2D living scene: a day/night sky with clouds, birds and stars; little animals walking and interacting on the grass (sprout, cat, bird, bunny); seasonal sakura / leaves / snow; and butterflies that follow your cursor.

## Accent & typography

- Settings › Appearance lets you pick an **accent color** for links, buttons and selection.
- Choose the body font (serif / sans), size and line height; heading colors are graded separately.

## Accessibility

- Settings › Effects can turn effects off, or follow the system "Reduce Motion" setting — when on, effects and the scene freeze to a single frame.
